# Article Writing Directive

## Goal
Write a 800–1200 word SEO-optimised blog article about a tech hardware topic in the CtrlAltStock brand voice.

## Tools
- `execution/ai_writer.py` — article generation
- `execution/ai_editor.py` — editorial pass

## Brand Voice
- UK English (colour, favourite, analyse)
- Enthusiastic but balanced (not a fanboy)
- Community-focused — reference the Discord
- Practical — always recommend something actionable
- Peer-to-peer tone — like advice from a friend who watches prices daily

## Article Structure (always follow)
1. H1: Compelling SEO title with product/topic name
2. Hook paragraph (why this matters NOW for bargain hunters)
3. H2: What's the story?
4. H2: What does this mean for prices?
5. H2: Our recommendation / What to buy right now
6. H2: Final thoughts + Discord CTA

## Required Elements
- At least 3 specific product names with model numbers
- Prices in GBP (£) where available, USD ($) otherwise
- Discord CTA: "Join our 10,000-member CtrlAltStock Discord"
- Bullet points and subheadings throughout
- JSON metadata block at the end (for slug, tags, Amazon queries)
