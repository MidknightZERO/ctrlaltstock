**Understanding VRR and Frame Gen**
To grasp the issue fully, it's crucial to understand both VRR and Frame Gen. VRR is a technology that allows the monitor to adjust its refresh rate dynamically to match the frame rate of the content being displayed. This synchronization prevents screen tearing, which occurs when the monitor's refresh rate and the game's frame rate are not in sync. Frame Gen, on the other hand, is Nvidia's technology aimed at improving performance in games by generating additional frames, especially in scenarios where the GPU is under heavy load. While both technologies are designed to enhance the gaming experience, their interaction can sometimes lead to unforeseen issues like the flickering observed with the RTX 5070 Ti and AW2725DF combination.
